## ğŸ“– Project Overview

**Kalavpp** is a comprehensive art commerce and creative services platform designed to bridge the gap between artists, art enthusiasts, and creative service seekers in India. The platform combines e-commerce functionality with creative services, offering a unique marketplace for physical art products, digital art, and professional creative services.

### ğŸ¯ Problem Statement & Solution

**Problem:** 
- Artists struggle to reach wider audiences and monetize their work effectively
- Art collectors find it difficult to discover authentic, curated artworks
- No unified platform exists for both art commerce and creative services in India

**Our Solution:**
Kalavpp provides a comprehensive platform that:
- Empowers 1,200+ artists to showcase and sell their work
- Offers 5,000+ curated artworks to collectors
- Provides custom commission services and art workshops
- Supports multiple user roles (customers, vendors, admins)
- Integrates Indian payment options (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, COD)
